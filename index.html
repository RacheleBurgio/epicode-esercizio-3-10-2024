Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia apetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie.It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image:Sally Vegas

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups(500g)all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups(350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

fpr the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. make the focaccia dough

    2. in a medium bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix togheter all the dough Ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed.
